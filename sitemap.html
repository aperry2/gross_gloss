12 Chapters
~~~~~~~~~~~
A complete hypertext edition of all twelve chapters, organized for quick access through a simple scrollable menu. Each chapter reproduces the original structure while offering a clear, screen-optimized reading view. Users may navigate linearly or explore individual sections independently.

Gloss Images
~~~~~~~~~~~~
A curated gallery of the manuscript’s illuminations, high-key scans, and restored embellishments. Each image has been optimized for 16-color displays and may appear slightly different from print versions. Use your browser’s built-in navigation to move between entries.

Supplemental Documentation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
A selection of supporting materials, early design tests, and archival drafts scanned from manuscripts in various collections. These files offer insight into the creative and technical development of the Gross Gloss. Some documents include alternate layouts not present in the final edition.

About
~~~~~
Background information on the manuscript, its provenance, and the methods used to produce this digital release. This section explains how historical source materials were adapted for modern systems. Users seeking context before reading will find a concise orientation here.

Constraints & Research
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
An overview of the procedural rules, structural limits, and research references guiding the creation of the Gross Gloss. This section details the numeric and symbolic conditions under which each chapter was composed. It also lists the primary archives and theoretical frameworks consulted during development.
